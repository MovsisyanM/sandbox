### Problem 1:

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} + y^2 sinx = 0, \ \ \ \ y(0) = 1 $$

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = -y^2 sinx $$

$$ \frac{dy}{y^2} = -sinx dx $$

$$ \int_1^y\frac{dy}{y^2} = \int_0^x-sinx dx $$

$$ \left.-\frac{1}{y} \right|_1^y = cosx $$
