### Separable first-order equations
These are the ones where you can drag the y terms on one side and x terms on the other, forming  
$$ g(y)dy = f(x)dx $$  

Solution:
$$ \int_{y_0}^yg(y)dy = \int_{x_0}^xf(x)dx $$
